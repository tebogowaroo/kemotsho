## 4. Our Services

At the heart of Waroo is our modular technical foundation. We provide end-to-end agency services to deploy, customize, and extend these core engines:

### The Content Management System (CMS) Engine
A modern, headless-style CMS built directly into your platform. 
*   **Dynamic Page Builder:** Create rich layouts, landing pages, and marketing content without touching code.
*   **Media Management:** Securely host, organize, and serve your digital assets.
*   **Role-Based Publishing:** Robust author and editor roles ensure your content pipeline is secure and structured.
*   **Seamless Navigation:** Control your site's structure, menus, and internal linking dynamically.

### The E-Commerce Engine
A complete, scalable retail backbone designed for modern commerce.
*   **Product & Marketing Management:** Handle complex product variants, categories, and marketing campaigns.
*   **End-to-End Checkout:** Integrated carts, high-conversion checkout flows, and payment gateway webhooks (Payfast, Stripe).
*   **Customer & Order Portals:** Comprehensive admin dashboards for inventory, alongside dedicated portals for customers to manage their order history.
*   **Deep Analytics:** Built-in reporting workflows to track sales, traffic, and conversions.

### The Medical Practice Engine
A secure, patient-centric module designed specifically for healthcare providers.
*   **Appointment Scheduling:** Real-time booking systems with integrations for medical slot management (e.g., Recomed).
*   **Patient Portal:** Secure environments for patients to manage their profiles, view history, and interact with practitioners.
*   **Strict Security & Permissions:** Built firmly on secure server-side logic to ensure sensitive healthcare data is never exposed.
*   **Administrative Dashboard:** Streamline front-desk operations with a comprehensive view of daily appointments and patient records.

### Bespoke Development Services
Every business is distinct. Waroo acts as your dedicated agency to build customized features on top of our core engines. Whether you need a bespoke internal workflow, a unique third-party integration, or a custom user interface, our engineers build it securely and perfectly aligned with your business model.
